Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FocusLab, a 24/7 coworking space in Talisay City, Cebu.

Where is FocusLab coworking space located?

FocusLab is located at Southscape Commercial Building, 2nd Floor Unit B1-2-4A, Lawa-an 1, Talisay City, Cebu — at the back of Mani Mogo Restaurant. We're easy to find in Southscape, just south of Cebu City.

Is FocusLab open 24/7?

Yes, FocusLab is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Drop in any time, day or night, whether you need a quiet morning session or a late-night Night Pass.

What membership plans does FocusLab offer?

We offer flexible pay-as-you-go passes (hourly, 3-hour, and day) plus Weekly, 10-Day, Monthly, and Dedicated Monthly memberships. Each plan is also available as a Night Pass for after-hours access.

Does FocusLab have a Night Pass?

Yes, FocusLab offers a Night Pass for after-hours access starting 9:00 PM onwards. Night Pass is not available for walk-ins — it requires advance booking at least 1 day prior and is only available for guests on at least a Weekly plan (Weekly, 10-Day, or Monthly).

Is FocusLab good for freelancers and remote workers on US time zones?

Definitely. Since we're open 24/7, freelancers, VAs, and remote workers who follow US client hours or work the night shift can book a Night Pass and get a quiet, air-conditioned space whenever their workday actually happens.

How much does a weekly or monthly plan cost?

A Weekly Pass is ₱2,500 (₱3,500 for the Weekly Night Pass), and a Monthly Membership is ₱6,500 (₱8,000 for the Monthly Night Pass). Check our Promos page for current limited-time discounts.

Does FocusLab have private workspaces?

Yes. Our Dedicated Monthly and Dedicated Night Pass plans give you your own dedicated workspace instead of shared seating, so the same spot is reserved for you.

Is FocusLab the first coworking space in South Cebu?

FocusLab is one of the newest 24/7 coworking spaces serving Talisay City and the wider South Cebu area, built for the area's growing community of freelancers, remote workers, VAs, and students who used to commute to Cebu City for a proper workspace.

How do I book a plan at FocusLab?

You can book directly on our Book Now page. Pick a pass or plan, choose your date and time, and pay via GCash or at the counter when you arrive. Advance booking is recommended since slots are limited.

Does FocusLab have parking, wifi, and air conditioning?

FocusLab has fast, stable internet and a fully air-conditioned workspace as standard. Southscape Commercial Building has a vast free parking space area, so parking is never an issue when you visit.
